Internet of Things
IoT affects our everyday lives without us knowing. It is a key design concept of most modern devices and allows us to use devices without worrying about their inter-connectivity. It refers to how modern devices connect to each other in a way that humans need not interact, improving automation and hence improving most aspects of our lives.
